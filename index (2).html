The Vocabulario of Life

Blog educativo visual para aprender palabras avanzadas en inglés con imágenes.

Serendipity

The occurrence of events by chance in a happy or beneficial way.

Ephemeral

Lasting for a very short time.

Ineffable

Too great or extreme to be expressed in words.

Ethereal

Extremely delicate and light in a way that seems too perfect for this world.

Petrichor

A pleasant smell that frequently accompanies the first rain after a long period of warm, dry weather.

Solitude

The state of being alone without being lonely.

Iridescent

Showing luminous colors that seem to change when seen from different angles.

Mellifluous

A sound that is pleasingly smooth and musical to hear.

Euphoria

A feeling or state of intense excitement and happiness.

Resilience

The capacity to recover quickly from difficulties; toughness.

Quintessential

Representing the most perfect or typical example of a quality or class.

Labyrinthine

Like a labyrinth; irregular and twisting.

Sonorous

An imposingly deep and full sound.

Luminous

Emitting or reflecting light, especially in the dark.

Epiphany

A moment of sudden and great revelation or realization.
